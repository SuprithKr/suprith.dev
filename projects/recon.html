Network Recon & Packet Analysis

Tools: Kali Linux, Nmap, Wireshark

Scenario

Performed reconnaissance on a lab network to simulate the information-gathering phase of a security assessment and identify potential attack surfaces relevant to SOC monitoring.

What I Did

Used Nmap to scan the lab network for active hosts, open ports, and running services. Captured live traffic using Wireshark to observe network behavior during the scan.

What I Analyzed

Identified exposed services and open ports that could represent attack vectors. Detected abnormal traffic patterns in the packet capture that would trigger alerts in a SOC environment.

Decision Made

Documented exposed services with recommended hardening actions. Noted which findings would be flagged as suspicious in a SIEM and what the appropriate SOC response would be for each pattern identified.
