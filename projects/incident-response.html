Kali Linux Wireshark Incident Response
Scenario

Simulated a brute-force attack against a lab environment to practice identifying indicators of compromise and executing a structured incident response. The objective was to detect, contain, and document the incident end-to-end.

Tools Used

Kali Linux was used to generate the brute-force attack traffic in a controlled lab environment. Wireshark was used to capture and analyze the resulting network packets in real time during and after the simulated attack.

What I Analyzed

Identified indicators of compromise including repeated failed authentication attempts, unusual connection frequencies, and abnormal packet behavior. Isolated the attack source and reconstructed the full attack timeline from the packet capture data.

Decision Made

Drafted a structured incident response plan covering containment steps, evidence preservation procedures, recovery actions, and post-incident recommendations. Documented the full timeline from initial detection through to resolution and lessons learned.
