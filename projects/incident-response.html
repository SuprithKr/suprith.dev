Incident Response Simulation

Tools: Kali Linux, Wireshark

Scenario

Simulated a brute-force attack against a lab environment to practice identifying indicators of compromise and executing a structured incident response.

What I Did

Generated brute-force attack traffic in a controlled lab setting. Captured network packets using Wireshark and analyzed the traffic to identify attack patterns and anomalies.

What I Analyzed

Identified indicators of compromise including repeated failed authentication attempts, unusual connection frequencies, and abnormal packet behavior. Isolated the attack source and timeline from the packet capture.

Decision Made

Drafted a structured incident response plan covering containment steps, evidence preservation, recovery actions, and post-incident recommendations. Documented the full timeline from detection to resolution.
