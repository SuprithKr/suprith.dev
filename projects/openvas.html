OpenVAS Vulnerability Assessment

Tools: OpenVAS, Linux VM

Scenario

Simulated a vulnerability assessment on an internal lab network to identify and prioritize security weaknesses before they could be exploited.

What I Did

Configured and ran a full OpenVAS scan against the lab environment. Reviewed the generated report, triaged findings by severity, and identified the highest-risk vulnerabilities requiring immediate attention.

What I Analyzed

Cross-referenced scan results against known CVEs. Prioritized findings based on CVSS scores and business impact. Mapped critical findings to ISO 27001 control A.12.6.1 — Technical Vulnerability Management.

Decision Made

Produced a prioritized remediation list with recommended actions for each high and critical finding. Documented findings in a structured report format suitable for internal audit review.
