Security Project

OpenVAS Vulnerability Assessment

Full vulnerability scan on a lab environment — prioritized findings mapped to ISO 27001.

OpenVAS Linux VM ISO 27001
Scenario

Simulated a vulnerability assessment on an internal lab network to identify and prioritize security weaknesses before they could be exploited. The goal was to produce a structured report suitable for internal audit review.

Tools Used

OpenVAS running on a Linux VM was used to conduct the full scan. The scan was configured to target the lab environment's IP range and run a comprehensive check against known CVEs and misconfigurations.

What I Analyzed

Cross-referenced scan results against known CVEs. Triaged findings by CVSS score and business impact. Identified high and critical severity items requiring immediate remediation. Mapped critical findings to ISO 27001 control A.12.6.1 — Technical Vulnerability Management.

Decision Made

Produced a prioritized remediation list with recommended actions for each high and critical finding. Structured the output as a formal report with severity ratings, affected systems, and remediation timelines — formatted for internal audit consumption.
